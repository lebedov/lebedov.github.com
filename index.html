About Me

I am currently pursuing a PhD in computational neuroscience at Columbia University's Department of Electrical Engineering under the supervision of Professor Aurel A. Lazar. You can read more about my academic interests on my research page and peruse some of my past projects on my projects page.

I'm also an active volunteer contributor to the Mandriva Linux distribution; I maintain a large number of (mostly scientific) software packages, many of which I use in the course of my research.
