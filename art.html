My Online Gallery

My scientific research interests notwithstanding, I am fond of calligraphy and illustration. The examples listed this page constitute a selection of some of my more interesting compositions. This list is shorter than it could have been owing to my tendency to give away compositions as gifts and discard quick drawings before I come within range of a scanner.

The resolution of these images has been reduced to speed up image loading over slow Internet connections. Being that the pictures are displayed at the same scale, I have indicated the actual dimensions in the description displayed alongside each image.

Please note that all of the illustrations on my site are copyrighted and hence not in the public domain. If you wish to copy or make use of them for some purpose, please contact me.
