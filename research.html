Research

My current research focuses on spike-domain processing of video signals using massively parallel networks of neuron models. These activities are part of a larger effort by my advisor's research group to shed light on how the brain processes information by employing analytic and computational approaches grounded in fundamental results from digital and analog signal processing.

Software written by myself and my colleagues as part of our research activities may be downloaded from our group's GitHub repository. Other open source software that I have written is available on my public code page hosted by GitHub.
