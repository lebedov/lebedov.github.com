Research

My current research focuses on time-domain signal processing inspired by novel neural information representation models. These activities are part of a larger effort by my advisor's research group to address questions in theoretical neuroscience by advancing analytic and computational approaches grounded in fundamental results from digital and analog signal processing.

Software written by myself and my colleagues as part of our research activities may be downloaded from our group's online repository. Other open source software that I have written is available on my public code page hosted by GitHub.
