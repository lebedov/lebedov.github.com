Research

My current research focuses on

  • spike-domain processing of large-scale signals using massively parallel networks of neuron models;
  • functional emulation of the brain of the fruit fly Drosophila melanogaster on clusters of Graphics Processing Units (GPUs);
  • reverse engineering the fruit fly brain by functional verification of emulated brain modules.

These activities are part of a larger effort by my advisor's research group to shed light on how the brain processes information by employing analytic and computational approaches grounded in fundamental results from digital and analog signal processing.

Software written by myself and my colleagues at the Bionet Group as part of our research activities may be downloaded from our group's GitHub repository. Other open source software and useful code snippets that I have written are respectively available on my GitHub repository and gist pages.
